# Privacy and Data Handling

## Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email address, payment information, account preferences), information generated by your use of the service (usage logs, feature interaction data, query history), and technical information about your device and connection (IP address, browser type, OS version). We do not collect the content of private files you store unless you explicitly share them with support for troubleshooting purposes.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to operate the service, process payments, provide customer support, detect and prevent abuse, and improve the product. We use aggregated and anonymized usage data for internal analytics and product development. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes on external platforms.

## Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, personal data is purged within 90 days, except for financial records (retained for 7 years as required by law), anonymized analytics data (retained indefinitely in aggregate form), and records related to active disputes or legal matters.

## Third-Party Processors

We share data with a limited set of third-party processors necessary to operate the service: our payment processor (Stripe) handles payment data, our email delivery service handles outbound email, and our cloud infrastructure provider hosts all service data. All processors are contractually bound to handle data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and may not use your data for their own purposes.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and object to certain types of processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@example.com. We respond to rights requests within 30 days.

## Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We use optional analytics cookies to understand how the product is used in aggregate. You can opt out of analytics cookies through the cookie settings panel in the platform.
