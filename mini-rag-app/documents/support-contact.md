# Support Contact and Response Times

## Available Support Channels

We offer support through three channels: live chat, email, and a community forum. Live chat is available 24/7 for customers on paid plans. Email support is available to all customers, including those on the free tier. The community forum is a public resource monitored by our team and experienced community members.

## Response Time by Plan

Free tier customers can expect an initial email response within 3 business days. Starter plan customers receive a response within 1 business day. Professional and Business plan customers receive a response within 4 hours during business hours (Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time). Enterprise customers have a dedicated account manager and a contractual SLA — refer to your agreement for the exact response time.

## Live Chat

Live chat is available from the in-app help widget. If no agent is available, the chat converts to an email ticket automatically. Chat transcripts are emailed to you after the conversation ends. Live chat is not available for free tier customers.

## What to Include in a Support Request

Include your account ID or the email address associated with your account in every request. For billing issues, include the transaction ID or the date and amount of the charge. For technical issues, include the error message you are seeing, the browser or client version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Requests with sufficient context are resolved significantly faster than those without.

## Escalation

If you are unsatisfied with the resolution of a support ticket, you can request escalation to a senior support agent by replying to the ticket with "Escalation requested." Escalated tickets are reviewed within 1 business day regardless of plan tier.
