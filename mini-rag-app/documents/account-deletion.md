# Account Deletion and Data Retention

## How to Request Account Deletion

Account deletion requests can be submitted through Settings > Account > Delete Account, or by emailing privacy@example.com with your registered email address and account ID. Requests submitted via email are processed within 5 business days. Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible — we cannot recover data after deletion is complete.

## What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove your personal profile information, contact details, payment method tokens (not the underlying payment method — that is managed by our payment processor), and all content you created within the platform. Associated data such as activity logs and usage records are anonymized rather than deleted outright, so aggregate statistics derived from them may persist in an anonymized form.

## Data Retention After Deletion

Some data is retained after account deletion as required by law or legitimate business interest. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations. Records related to unresolved disputes or pending legal matters are retained until the matter is resolved. Backup copies of data may persist for up to 90 days after deletion before being purged from our backup systems.

## Active Subscriptions and Billing

You cannot delete your account while an active paid subscription is in place. You must cancel your subscription first and allow it to lapse, then request deletion. Any outstanding invoices must also be settled before deletion is processed. Attempting to delete an account with an active subscription will result in an error message directing you to the cancellation flow.

## Third-Party Data

If your account data has been shared with authorized third-party integrations you connected, we cannot guarantee deletion of copies held by those third parties. You should separately review and revoke access for any third-party apps before deleting your account.
