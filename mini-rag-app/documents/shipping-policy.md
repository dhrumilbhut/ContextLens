# Shipping Policy

## Standard Delivery Timeframes

Orders placed on business days before 2:00 PM local time are typically dispatched the same day. Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days for domestic orders within the continental United States. Expedited shipping (2–3 business days) and overnight shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee. Delivery timeframes are estimates and not guarantees — carrier delays beyond our control may affect actual delivery dates.

## International Shipping

We ship to most countries in Europe, Asia-Pacific, and North America. International orders typically take 10–21 business days depending on the destination country and customs processing times. The customer is responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees levied by the destination country — these are not included in the shipping fee charged at checkout and will not be refunded.

## Shipping to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO Addresses

Standard shipping is available to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses. Expedited and overnight shipping options are not available for these address types. Delivery timeframes to APO/FPO addresses may extend to 21 business days.

## Delays and Lost Packages

If tracking information shows no movement for more than 5 business days, contact support with your order number to initiate an investigation. For orders confirmed lost by the carrier, we will reship the order at no additional cost or issue a full refund at your preference. We are not liable for packages marked as delivered but reported missing — in these cases, check with neighbors and your local carrier facility before contacting us.

## Order Tracking

A tracking number is emailed to the address on file within 24 hours of dispatch. Tracking updates may take up to 48 hours to appear in the carrier's system after the initial scan. If you did not receive a tracking email, check your spam folder or contact support to have it resent.
