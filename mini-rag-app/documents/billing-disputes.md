# Billing Disputes

## How to Dispute a Charge

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, contact our billing team within 60 days of the charge appearing on your statement. Disputes submitted after 60 days may not be eligible for investigation or refund. To open a dispute, go to Settings > Billing > Transaction History and click "Dispute" next to the charge in question, or email billing@example.com with your account ID, the charge date, and the amount in question.

## Dispute Investigation Timeframe

We aim to acknowledge billing disputes within 2 business days and resolve them within 10 business days. For complex cases — such as disputes involving multiple charges over an extended period, or cases where the charge originated from a third-party integration — resolution may take up to 20 business days. You will receive a status update at each stage of the investigation.

## Common Dispute Reasons and Outcomes

Charges resulting from our error (duplicate charge, incorrect plan price, charge after a confirmed cancellation) are refunded in full without question. Charges resulting from a renewal you forgot to cancel are reviewed on a case-by-case basis — if the subscription renewed automatically and you contact us within 7 days of the renewal charge, we will typically issue a refund as a courtesy. Beyond 7 days, renewals are generally non-refundable.

## Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback through your bank or credit card issuer without first contacting us may result in your account being suspended pending resolution. We strongly encourage direct dispute resolution before escalating to a chargeback, as we can usually resolve billing issues faster than a bank dispute process. If a chargeback is found in our favor, a chargeback fee may be applied to your account.

## Currency and Exchange Rate Disputes

We charge in US dollars. If your bank converts the charge to a local currency, exchange rate fluctuations between the charge date and your statement date are not billing errors and are not eligible for dispute.
