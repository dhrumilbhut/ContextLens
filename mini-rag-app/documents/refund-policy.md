# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of their original purchase date for most products and services. To initiate a refund, contact our support team with your order number and the reason for the request. Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method.

## Digital Goods and Software Licenses

Digital products, downloadable software, and one-time software licenses are non-refundable once the product has been downloaded or the license key has been revealed. If you encounter a technical issue that prevents you from using the product, contact support before requesting a refund — we will attempt to resolve the issue first. Exceptions may be granted at our discretion if the product was misrepresented in its description.

## Subscription Refunds

For monthly subscriptions, we do not issue prorated refunds for the remainder of the billing period after cancellation. The subscription remains active until the end of the paid period. For annual subscriptions, customers who cancel within the first 14 days are eligible for a full refund. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable but the subscription remains active for the full 12-month period.

## Damaged or Defective Physical Goods

Physical goods that arrive damaged or defective are eligible for a full refund or replacement, regardless of when the issue is reported, provided the damage is not the result of customer misuse. Photographic evidence of the damage must be submitted with the refund request. Return shipping for defective items is covered by us.

## Non-Refundable Items

The following are never eligible for refunds: gift cards, setup or onboarding fees, custom development work completed and delivered, and any service fees already incurred during the refund window. Fees charged by third-party payment processors may not be recoverable even when a refund is issued.
